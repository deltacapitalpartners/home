About Us



Delta Capital Partners is an independent investment bank specializing in the Greater Mekong Sub-region (GMS). We provide strategic advice and world-class solutions to an exclusive clientele. We are committed to the long-term success of our clients and we measure our performance by it.

Our Values


Integrity
 We firmly believe in providing objective advice centered around our clients’ best interest. We actively avoid any activities that may compromise these interests.

Excellence
 We strive to provide our clients with customised financial services. Our team’s international experience brings global perspective and high institutional standards to all our partnerships.

Commitment
 We dedicate ourselves to building a long-term partnership with our clients by thoroughly understanding their business needs before tailoring quality solutions.

Our Services

M&A and Strategic Advisory



We work with management to understand their vision and planning. In advising companies, we evaluate potential acquisition targets, provide valuation analyses, and evaluate and propose financial and strategic alternatives. We also advise as to the timing, structure, financing, and pricing of a proposed acquisition, and assist in negotiating and closing the deal.


We advise clients contemplating the sale of certain businesses, assets, or their entire company by evaluating and recommending financial and strategic alternatives with respect to the sale, advising on the appropriate sales process for the situation and valuation issues, and preparing an offering memorandum and other appropriate sales materials. We also identify and contact qualified acquirers and assist in negotiating and closing the sale.

Capital Advisory


We advise clients on a broad range of strategic and tactical issues, including capital structure optimization, capital allocation, equity and debt market positioning and issuance strategies.


We advise private companies in the placement of senior debt and subordinated debt, convertible securities and private equity.


We work with management to develop internal financial systems, controls and reporting procedure that will lead to good governance. We provide innovative, objective and timely advice on all aspects of raising equity capital, including valuation, underwriter selection, incentive structuring, investor targeting, syndicate structuring, security structuring, and offering size and pricing.


Our team advises and negotiates on behalf of clients regarding alternatives for existing debt and the issuance of new debt.

Financial Advisory


As an independent third party we can assist financial institutions in sales, re-structuring or refinancing of non-performing loan assets. We will work with financial institutions and clients to find creative and mutually agreeable solutions.


We offer practical advice to enact corporate governance strategies to raise local corporate governance practice to international standard. We have many years of experience in guiding companies in implementing proper corporate governance. We assist companies in implementing financial controls, systems and procedure, purchasing authorizations, and audits and oversights.
