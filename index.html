Tarek Genena
Founder

Mr. Genena is a mergers and acquisitions specialist. He has been involved in over 30 transactions from small and medium enterprises to divisions of NYSE companies. In addition Mr. Genena has hands on operating management experience in corporate restructurings, refinancing, and recapitalizations. He brings with him both a traditional and creative approach to financing in the most difficult transactional environments. In addition he is a turnaround specialist and can assist clients in formulation of current and future strategies for building a solid foundation to grow. Most recently in Cambodia he has been active with the private equity group VTrust and routinely works with all the participants in the IPO market from the SECC to the underwriters, distributors, and valuations. Companies. Mr. Genena has a particular interest in Micro-finance, commercial banking and securitization transactions. He is currently a consultant to VTrust Appraisals co, Ltd—an SECC approved Valuations Company—and is a member of CIC the Cambodian Investment Club.
